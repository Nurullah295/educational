Acne vulgaris:
is the formation of comedones, papules, pustules, nodules, and/or cysts as a result of obstruction and inflammation of pilosebaceous units.

The retinoids are a class of chemical compounds that are vitamers of vitamin A or are chemically related to it. Retinoids have found use in medicine where they regulate epithelial cell growth.

Medical definition of vitamer:
any of two or more compounds that relieve a particular vitamin deficiency; also : a structural analog of a vitamin.

Holy Grail:
something that you want very much but that is very hard to get or achieve. : the cup that is said to have been used by Jesus Christ and that was sought by knights during the Middle Ages.(পানপাত্র)

Puberty:
Puberty is when a child's body begins to develop and change as they become an adult.(বয়ঃসন্ধি)

Androgens:
Androgens are hormones that contribute to growth and reproduction in both men and women. Androgens are usually thought of as male hormones, but the female body naturally produces a small amount of androgens too. Androgen deficiency in women is a controversial concept.
An androgen is any natural or synthetic steroid hormone that regulates the development and maintenance of male characteristics in vertebrates by binding to androgen receptors.

Bacteria colonization:
Bacteria colonization is the presence of bacteria on a body surface (like on the skin, mouth, intestines or airway) without causing disease in the person.

Keratinization:
Keratinization is a word pathologists use to describe cells producing large amounts of a protein called keratin. Cells that produce keratin are stronger than other cells which makes them good at forming a barrier between the outside world and in the inside of the body.
