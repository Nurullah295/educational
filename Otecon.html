Prescribing Information:
Composition:
Otecon Capsule: Each capsule contains Otes econazole INN 150 mg.
Pharmacology:
Oteseconazole is an azole metalloenzyme inhibitor targeting the fungal sterol, 14 α demethylase (CYP51), an enzyme that catalyzes an early step in the biosynthetic pathway of ergosterol. Ergosterol is required for fungal cell membrane formation and integrity. Inhibition of CYP51 results in the accumulation of 14-methylated sterols, some of which are toxic to fungi. Through the inclusion of a tetrazole metal-binding group, Oteseconazole has a lower affinity for human CYP enzymes.
