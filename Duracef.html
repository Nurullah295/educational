4. What do you mean by switch therapy ?
It means changing from an intravenous to an oral antibiotic during the management of an infectious disease . Switching from intravenous ( IV ) to oral ( PO ) therapy as soon as patients are clinically stable can reduce the length of hospitaliza tion and lower associated costs .
