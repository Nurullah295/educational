Prescribing Information
Composition:
Sulderm Cream:
Each gram cream contains Sulconazole Nitrate USP 10 mg.
Pharmacology:
Sulconazole nitrate is an imidazole derivative with broad-spectrum antifungal activity that inhibits the growth in vitro of the common pathogenic dermatophytes including Trichophyton rubrum, Trichophyton mentagrophytes, Epidermophyton floccosum and Microsporum canis. It also inhibits (in vitro) the organism responsible for tinea versicolor, Malassezia furfur.
Indication:
Sulderm Cream is indicated for the treatment of Tinea pedis(athlete's foot), Tinea cruris, Tinea corporis & Pityriasis versicolor.Tinea pedis(athlete's foot), Tinea cruris, Tinea corporis & Pityriasis versicolor.
Dose & Administration:
Tinea pedis(athlete's foot), Tinea cruris, Tinea corporis & Pityriasis versicolor. A small amount of cream should be gently massaged into the affected and surrounding skin areas once or twice daily, in case of Tinea Pedis where administration should be twice. daily. Early relief of symptoms is experienced by the majority of patients and clinical improvement may be seen fairly soon after treatment is begun; however, Tinea corporis/cruris and Tinea versicolor should be treated for 3 weeks and Tinea pedis for 4 weeks to reduce the possibility of recurrence. Or as directed by the physician.
Contraindication:
Sulconazole nitrate cream, is contraindicated in patients who have a history of hypersensitivity to any of its ingredients.
Warnings and Precautions:
Sulconazole nitrate cream, 1.0% is for external use only. Avoid contact with the eyes. If irritation develops, the cream should be discontinued and appropriate therapy instituted.
Side Effects:
There were no systemic effects and only infrequent cutaneous adverse reactions in 1185 patients treated with Sulconazole nitrate cream in controlled clinical trials. Approximately 3% of these patients reported itching, 3% burning and 1% redness. These complaints did not usually interfere with treatment.
Use in Pregnancy and Lactation:
The use of Sulconazole is not safe for pregnant women. Use in Children and Adolescents. Safety and effectiveness in children have not been established.
Drug Interaction:
There are no data available. Overdose:
There are no data available.
Storage:
Store below 30°C, protected from light & in a dry place. Keep all medicines out of the reach of children.
