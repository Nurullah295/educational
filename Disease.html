Gout:
• Gout is a painful form of arthritis that occurs when high levels of uric acid in the blood cause crystals to form and accumulate in and around a joint.
• It is more likely to affect men
• Affect women after the menopause

Symptoms:
• Intense joint pain that subsides to discomfort, inflammation and redness

Treatment:
• NSAIDs & Corticosteroids
• Uricosuric agents: Allopurinol, Probenecid
• Urinary Alkaliniser
