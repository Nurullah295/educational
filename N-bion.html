    Disease Profile:
  • Diabetic neuropathy:
    • Nerve damage caused by diabetes that leads to numbness and sometimes pain and weakness in the hands, arms, feet, and legs.
  • Neuritis:
    • Inflammation in nerve & pain in neuron causing loss of nerve function.
  • Neuralgia:
    • Burning sensation causing stabbing & severe pain.
  • Lumbago:
    • Lumbago is a general term used to describe pain in the lower back.
  • Sciatica:
    • Pain affecting the back hip and outer side of the leg caused by compression of spinal nerve.
  • Myalgia:
    • Myalgia describes muscle aches and pain, which can involve ligaments, tendons and fascia, the soft tissues that connect muscles, bones and organs.
  • Trigeminal neuralgia:
    • Trigeminal neuralgia is a chronic pain condition that affects the trigeminal nerves, which carry sensation from face to brain & leading to facial paralysis.
