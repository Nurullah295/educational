Pain:
☞ Sensation of discomfort, distress resulting from the stimulation of specialized nerve endings.
According to Nature there are Two types of Pain —
☞ (1) Acute Pain: Associated with trauma or disease and usually has a well defined location, character and timing. This pain lasting less than six month
☞ (2) Chronic Pain: Usually regarded as pain lasting more than few month. It is not clearly associated with trauma or disease or may persist after the injury has healed

Arthritis:
☞ Inflammation of a joint or joints causing pain or disability, swelling and stiffness due to various causes such as infection, trauma, degenerative changes or disorders.

Osteoarthritis:
☞ Osteoarthritls is a condition of joints inflammation. It also known as degenerative joint disease (DJD), as cartilage of joint becomes gradually decreases. Osteoarthritis is the second commonest musculoskeletal problem in the world.

Rheumatoid arthritis:
☞ Rheumatoid arthritis is an autoimmune disease that causes chronic inflammation of the joints.

Ankylosing Spondylitis:
☞ Ankylosing spondylitis is an inflammatory disease of the spine. Affects men three to four times more than women.
