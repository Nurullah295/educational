    2. Write down the brand taglines, indications & dosage guidelines of Itopri?

    Ans: The brand tagline of Itopri is a novel gastroprokinetics. Itopri is indicated for Dyspepsia, Indigestion, Anorexia, GERD, Heartburn, Nausea and Vomiting. Dosage guidelines of Itopri is one tablet three (3) times daily before a meal.

    3. Write down the mechanism of action of Itopri?

    Ans: Itopride is a new polyurethane agent and acts both as a dopamine D2 receptor antagonist and as an acetylcholine esterase inhibitor. Dopamine D2 receptor antagonism removes the inhibitory relief on acetylcholine release. The inhibition of enzyme acetylcholinesterase prevents degradation of acetylcholine. The net effect is an increase in acetylcholine concentration, promoting gastric motility, accelerating gastric emptying and having an anti-enetic action.

Dyspepsia affects up to 80% of the population at some time in life. Symptoms of Dyspepsia are early satiety, postprandial fullness, bloating, Epigastric pain, Epigastric burning, Nausea, Belching and heartburn

    Prescribing Information:
    Composition:
    Itopri 50 Tablet:
    Each film coated tablet contains Itopride Hydrochloride INN 50 mg.
    Pharmacology:
    Itopride Hydrochloride that increases the level of acetylcholine concentration, increasing the Gastrointestinal peristalsis movement, thereby accelerating gastric emptying time and easing the movement of food through the entire gastrointestinal tract. Itopride Hydrochloride also stops the sensation of vomiting/nausea by blocking the D2 receptors located in the chemoreceptor trigger zone of the brain.
    Indication:
    Itopride Hydrochloride is used in the treatment of gastrointestinal disorders like dyspepsia, bloating, upper abdominal pain, anorexia, heartburn, nausea and vomiting.
    Dosage & Administration:
    Adult:
    Recommended dose 50 mg three times a day before meal or as directed by the physicians.
    Contraindications:
    Itopride Hydrochloride is contraindicated in patients with known hypersensitivity to any other components of this drug.
    Warnings & Precautions:
    Itopride potentiates acetylcholine action and can induce cholinergic effects. Patients with reduced kidney or liver functions should be monitored carefully and the dose should be adjusted accordingly. Data about long-term administration of Itopride is not available.
    Side Effects:
    Common side effects are diarrhea, headache, nausea, dizziness, constipation, stomach pain.
    Use in Pregnancy & Lactation:
    The safety of this drug in pregnancy and lactation has not been established.
    Use in Children & Adolescents:
    Safety in children below 16 years of age has not been established.
    Drug Interaction:
    Itopride Hydrochloride may interact with anticholinergic agents (dicyclomine, trihexyphenidyl, ipratropium, belladonna alkaloids, benztropine mesylate, flavoxate, atropine, scopolamine and hyoscyamine).
    Overdosage:
    Data is not available.
    Storage:
    Store below 30°C, keep away from light and in a dry place. Keep all medicines out of the reach of children.
