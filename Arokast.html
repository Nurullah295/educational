☞ Asthma Asthma is a chronic, inflammatory diseases of air ways.


Typical symptoms of asthma are-
1. Shortness of breath
2. Wheeze
3. Chest tightness
4. Cough


☞ Factors of Asthma
Three main factors cause the airways to become narrow:
1. The muscles around the airways Bronchoconstriction)
2. The inside lining of the airways becomes red and swollen (Inflammation)
3. Extra mucus (sticky fluid) may be produced, blocking small airways


• Asthma Diagnosis
• Medical and family history
• Physical examination
• Lung function tests (Spiro metry)

☞ Asthma Triggers
• Cold
• Exercise/Increased physical activity
• Inhaled allergens (e.g. pollens, animal dander and dust mites)
• Changes in temperature and weather
• Environmental factors (e.g. dust, pollution, wood smoke etc.)
• Certain medications (e.g. aspirin)
• Chemicals and strong smells (e.g. perfumes, deodorants)
