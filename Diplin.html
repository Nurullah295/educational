Diabetes?:
Diabetes is a group of metabolic disorder in which persistent hyperglycaemia is caused by-
☞ Deficient insulin secretion or
☞ Resistance to the action of insulin


Common symptoms of Diabetes:
☞ Frequent urination.
☞ Unintended weight loss.
☞ Increased thirst & hunger.
☞ Fatigue.




Diabetes is Manageable, But is not Curable
To maintain optimum blood glucose level, Diabetic patients needs:
☞ Diet+ Discipline+ Drug(3D Theory)
