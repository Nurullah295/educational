Mode of Action
Absorption
After oral administration of Prucalopride in healthy subjects, peak plasma concentration is achieved within 2-3 hours.
Activation
In the colon, Prucalopride selectively binds to and activates 5-HT4 receptors.
Release
Colonic 5-HT4 receptor activation facilitates the release of acetylcholine.
Move
Enhanced colonic peristalsis increases bowel motility.
