Dry Eye:
☞ Dry eye is defined as a disorder of tear film due to tear deficiency or excessive tear evaporation causing mild eye irritation to severe pain.

Tear:
☞ Tear is a drop of clear saline fluid secreted by the lacrimal gland.lt makes the surface of our eyes smooth and clear, and it helps protect our eyes from infection.

Common causes of decreased Tear production:
☞ Aging.
☞ Certain medical conditions, including diabetes, rheumatoid arthritis, Sjogren’s syndrome, thyroid disorders and vitamin A deficiency.
☞ Certain medications, including antihistamines, hormone replacement therapy, antidepressants, and drugs for high bloods pressure, acne, birth control and Parkinson’s disease.
☞ Laser eye surgery.
☞ Tear gland damage from inflammation or radiation.

Common causes of Increased tear evaporation:
☞ Wind, smoke or dry air.
☞ Blinking less often while reading, driving or working at a computer.
☞ Eyelid problems, such as out-turning of the lids (ectropion) and in-turning of the lids (entropion).

Tear film:
☞Tear film is the most anterior refracting surface of the eye. With each blink, an optically smooth tear film is spread across the surface of the cornea.

Function:
☞ Lubricates the eyelids.
☞ Carrying waste products away from the cornea.
☞ Prevents evaporation.
☞ Wetting of cornea.
Problems with any of these layers can cause dry eyes. For example, the oil film produced by small glands on the edge of eyelids (meibomian glands) might become clogged. Blocked meibomian glands are more common in people with inflammation along the edge oftieir eyelids (blepharitis).

Q. Why is Cfresh Liquigel highly effective drug for the management of dry eye?
Ans: Cfresh Liquigel is one kind of mid-viscosity artificial tear. Therefore, it increases the thickness of the tear film and retains water for long time. That's why Cfresh Liquigel is highly effective drug for the management of dry eye
Q. What is the basic difference between Liquigel and gel formulation?
Ans: Gel is so viscous that it does not properly disperse on corneal surface and hence it causes blurring & blurred vision which persists 1- 3 minutes. But Liquigel is similar to solution and it does not cause blurring & blurred vision like gel.
How Cfresh Liquigel can improve corneal wound healing effectively?
CMC (Cfresh Liquigel)
⇩
Binds with matrix proteins fibronectin or collagen
⇩
Forms CMC-fibronectin complex protein
⇩
Stimulates the epithelial cells and activate plasminogen
⇩
Produces plasminogen activator
⇩
Converts plasminogen to plasmin
⇩
Breaks down adhesions between cells and underlying subepithelial matrix
⇩
Improves epithelial wound healing
