Frequently Asked questions:

01. What is plaque psoriasis? What are the symptoms of plaque psoriasis?
Ans: Plaque psoriasis (psoriasis vulgaris) is a long-lasting (chronic) autoimmune disease that causes skin cells to reproduce very quickly. It causes thick, scaly patches called plaques on skin. The symptoms of plaque psoriasis include:
• Itchy skin
• Cracked, dry skin
• Skin pain.
• Redness of the skin

02. What are the major limitations of topical steroid preparations in the management of plaque psoriasis?
Ans: The major limitations of topical steroid preparations in the management of plaque psoriasis are:
• Skin thinning
• Skin pigmentation
• Stretch marks on the skin
• Redness
• Not suitable for long-term use
• Not recommended to use below 18 years of age
• Not recommended to use in the sensitive areas of the body (e.g. Under-arm, Under the breasts, Groin, stomach folds etc.)
